Bak Chor Mee

Description

Weâ€™re diving DEEP into a Teochew-style bak chor mee that BREAKS every single traditional rule!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Shrimp Chilli Paste
  • 1 tsp Black
  • 1.5 tbsp Light Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Shallot oil
  • 0.5 tsp MSG
  • 500 ml Pork Broth
  • 3 Fish Balls
  • 2 Meatballs
  • 7 slices Fish Cakes
  • 3 slices Prawns
  • 30g Minced Pork
  • 1 pack Mee kia
  • Small handful beansprouts
  • Lettuce
  • Spring Onion

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, add pork broth and bring to boil, add in meatballs and fishballs.
  2. In a serving bowl, add in light soy sauce, black vinegar, shrimp chilli paste, shallot oil, msg and mix well.
  3. Add fish cake, prawns and minced pork into the soup and cook for about 20 seconds. Set aside.
  4. Add in bean sprouts, mee kia/mee pok and cook for about 30-45 seconds. Transfer into serving bowl and mix well.
  5. Top with lettuce, cooked ingredients and spring onion.
