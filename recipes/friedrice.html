Fried Rice

Description

Lunar New Year is almost over but your fridge is filled with leftover ingredients like roast pork and vegetables… so we’re turning it into fried rice!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup Cooked Rice
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Long beans
  • Roast Pork
  • Fish Cake
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken Powder
  • White Pepper Powder
  • Ikan Bilis
  • Fried Garlic
  • Spring Onion

Steps

  1. Chop up left over ingredients, i.e. garlic, shallots, long beans, fish cake, etc.
  2. In a wok/pan over high heat, add 2 tbsp cooking oil. Crack in 1 egg and stir fry.
  3. Add in rice and stir fry until separated slightly. Push aside and add in garlic, shallots and stir fry until fragrant. Mix well.
  4. Add in roast pork, fish cake and stir fry. Mix well together.
  5. Add in long beans, season with soy, chicken powder and white pepper powder.
  6. Stir fry until everything is well mixed and separated.
  7. Add toppings of your liking or in your pantry and plate.
