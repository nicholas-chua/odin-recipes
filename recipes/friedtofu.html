Fried Tofu in Seafood Sauce

Description

Gold is more than a shiny color; it represents wealth, prosperity, and good fortune for the year aheadâ€”which makes these Tau Kwa Gold Bars with XO Sauce quite popular when Lunar New Year swings by.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Fortune Tau Kwa
  • Oil
  • 100g White shimeiji mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp Huatiao Wine
  • 3 tbsp Good Fortune XO Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 0.5 tsp White Pepper
  • 100 ml Chicken Stock
  • 150 ml Water
  • 2 tbsp Cornstarch Solution

Steps

  1. In wok pan with cooking oil, deep/shallow fry tau kwa until crispy and golden brown. Set aside.
  2. In the same wok with oil poured out, fry shimeiji mushrooms over medium heat.
  3. Deglaze with huatiao wine. Add Fortune XO sauce, abalone sauce, soy sauce white pepper and mix well.
  4. Bring to boil and add chicken stock and water. Bring to boil and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Adjust flavour to taste. Thicken with cornstarch solution, adding it bit by bit.
  6. Garnish with spring onion.
